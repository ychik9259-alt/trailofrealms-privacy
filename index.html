Privacy Policy for Trail of Realms

Last updated: 26 June 2026

AmiraStudio operates the mobile game Trail of Realms (the "Game").
This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection,
use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game.

1. Data We Collect
To provide online features (profile saving and synchronization),
we use Microsoft PlayFab. We collect:
- Your AppGallery player ID – to create and link your game account.
- Your chosen display name and selected avatar – to show your profile.
We do not collect your email, real name, phone number, or precise location.

2. In-App Purchases
The Game offers optional in-app purchases (items, currency). All payments
are processed by Huawei AppGallery. We do not receive or store your payment
card details. For more information, refer to Huawei’s privacy policy:
https://consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy/

3. Advertising
The Game may display advertisements provided by Huawei Ads. These ads are
optional (e.g., rewarded videos) and help support the Game. Huawei Ads may
use your advertising ID (OAID) and approximate device information to show
relevant ads. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of interest-based
ads in your device settings. For details, see Huawei Ads Privacy Policy:
https://consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy/

4. Children's Privacy
Our Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the
age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). If you believe your child has provided us
with personal information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

5. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your game profile and associated data by
contacting us at the email below. We will assist in removing your data 
from PlayFab in accordance with their procedures, typically within 30 days 
of your request.Please use "Privacy Request" as the email subject.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be
posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
trailofrealms@gmail.com
